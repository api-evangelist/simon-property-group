# Simon Property Group

Simon Property Group is the largest retail real estate investment trust (REIT) in the United States, owning, developing, and managing premier shopping, dining, entertainment, and mixed-use destinations. Simon's portfolio includes an interest in 254 properties: traditional malls, premium outlets, Mills centers, lifestyle centers, and other retail properties in North America, Europe, and Asia. The company operates ShopSimon, an e-commerce platform, and Simon Innovation Group to advance retail technology innovation.

**NYSE:** SPG (Fortune 500)

## APIs

### ShopSimon Retailer API

The ShopSimon Retailer Hub provides integration capabilities for sellers to connect product listings, inventory feeds, and order management with the ShopSimon marketplace. Integration is facilitated through the Rithum partner platform.

**Documentation:** https://sellersupport.shopsimon.com/hc/en-us/articles/9632618154647-API-Integration  
**Getting Started:** https://sellersupport.shopsimon.com/hc/en-us/articles/28034776101911-Getting-Started  

**Integration capabilities:**
- Product catalog synchronization
- Offer and inventory management
- Order fulfillment and management
- BOPIS (Buy Online, Pick Up In Store) support
